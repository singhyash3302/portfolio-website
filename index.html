Intro

Hello! I'm Yash Singh, computer science enthusiast who has recently completed the foundation level of BS degree in Data Science and it’s Applications at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM).

With a deep-rooted curiosity for technology and a drive to make a positive impact, I am pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in the dynamic field of computer science at the Institute of Engineering and Technology, Lucknow.

Work

Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a solid foundation in computer science concepts, programming languages, and software development methodologies. My coursework has equipped me with a strong understanding of algorithms, data structures, object-oriented programming, and web development. I am also an avid learner, constantly exploring new technologies and industry trends to stay up-to-date with the ever-evolving field of computer science.

As a team player, I value open communication, diversity of thought, and collective growth. I actively contribute my ideas while listening to others, fostering a collaborative environment where innovative ideas flourish.

Looking ahead, I am eager to embark on a career that allows me to leverage my technical expertise while continually expanding my skill set. I am particularly interested in opportunities that involve software development, data analysis, machine learning, or cybersecurity.
